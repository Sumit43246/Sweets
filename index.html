About Us

The taste of sugar is a highly desired taste around the world and sharing sweets among one another signifies happiness and celebration. Sweet taste gives a sense of happiness and satisfaction to most of us. The word “sweet” itself sound nice and pleasing to most of us, especially for children, but that's not all! The sweetness of sugar comes from a chemical interaction between sugar molecules and sweet taste receptor cells, which are found in our taste buds and on the roof of our mouth. Indian sweets are a type of confectionery that rely heavily on sugar, milk, flour and condensed milk. Although the bases of these sweets vary among regions, one thing is probably universal throughout the length and breadth of this vast country, it is the love for these colourful sweets which every Indian shares equally.
